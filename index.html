My tribute to coffee

One of life's pleasures

It is drunk when hot, it's drunk when cold

It is drunk by those, young and old

It is perfect in the morning, it can be consumed at night

It is sometimes had black, but also, on occasion, white

Sugar is added, if too bitter is the flavour

However it's taken, it's a taste to savour

Espresso, mocha, grande or cappuccino

Coffee is the best liquid tonic we know!
